Artemis is an ancient Greek mythological figure.
Known to in ancient Roman mythology as "Diana", she is the goddess of the hunt, the wilderness, wild animals, and nature, among other things. She was often heavily identified with Selene, the personifcation of the Moon, and Hecate - another lunar deity, and was thus regarded as one of the most prominent lunar deities in mythology.
